What Senior Interviews Expect

Design Skills

Ability to architect systems that handle millions of requests, with proper fault tolerance and scalability.

Trade-off Analysis

Understanding when to choose consistency over availability, or simplicity over scalability.

Leadership

Experience mentoring teams, driving technical decisions, and influencing engineering culture.

Incident Response

How you've handled production incidents, post-mortems, and built resilient systems.
